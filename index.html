Strategic Global Footprint

Where Infrastructure Meets Jurisdiction

Precision-located operational hubs & regulatory anchors across four continents.

🇬🇧 London, United Kingdom

Global headquarters situated in Canary Wharf – the most liquid timezone bridging Asian and American markets. Proximity to FCA, Bank of England and premier international arbitration courts grants real-time regulatory insight. Our core risk & HFT engines co-locate with LD4 Equinix, ensuring sub-100µs execution to tier-1 brokers. The common law jurisdiction provides the most sophisticated legal framework for algorithmic liability and smart contract enforcement, making London the undisputed nerve center for our global operations.

🇨🇲 Yaoundé, Cameroon (CEMAC Hub)

Strategic African anchor replacing legacy Nairobi presence. Yaoundé offers direct access to the CEMAC zone, stable Franc CFA monetary regime and emerging digital finance sandbox. ASI PRIME leverages its legaltech & regtech capabilities to structure central bank digital currency interoperability and compliance frameworks across the Economic Community of Central African States. Deep relationships with regional financial regulators position us for pan-African expansion. Low-latency fiber routes to West African undersea cables (SAT-3/WACS) ensure connectivity to Europe and Brazil. The city's growing fintech ecosystem and government-backed innovation hub reduce regulatory friction for cross-border payment systems.

🇺🇸 New York, USA

Quantitative trading nerve center co-located at NY5/NY4 data centers. Direct access to NYSE, NASDAQ, and IEX liquidity pools. Proximity to SEC and prime broker community enables pre-clearance algorithmic compliance under Reg SCI and Reg NMS. The Eastern time zone captures the overlap with London and the opening of Asian markets. Our behavioral finance unit processes SEC EDGAR filings and 13F disclosures in real-time to model institutional sentiment shifts. New York also serves as our primary hub for prime brokerage relationships and securities lending operations.

🇸🇬 Singapore

APAC flagship office for behavioral finance AI clustering and forex HFT. Monetary Authority of Singapore’s progressive fintech licensing and cross-border data governance align with our regulatory technology stacks. SG1 carrier-neutral connectivity ensures millisecond execution to Tokyo, Sydney, and Shanghai. Singapore's status as a leading FX center (average daily volume >$500B) provides ideal liquidity for our quant trading strategies. The city-state's robust IP protection laws and tax incentives for financial technology R&D make it a critical hub for our machine learning research team.

🇩🇪 Frankfurt, Germany

EU regulatory anchor hosting our LegalTech & auditing division. Located within Deutsche Börse's proximity, enabling seamless MIFID II/RTS 27 compliance. Hub for institutional smart contract auditing and ESG algorithmic validation under ESMA standards. Frankfurt's central European location offers low-latency access to Xetra, Euronext, and London via cross-border fiber. The city's deep pool of legal and compliance talent specialized in European financial regulation provides us with a competitive advantage for cross-border fund structuring and regulatory reporting automation.
