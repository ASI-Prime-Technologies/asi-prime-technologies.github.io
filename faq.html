Investor FAQ

What is the minimum investment?

For the flagship composite strategy: $5M. Lower minimums may be available for family offices or long‑term commitments.

What are the fees?

Management fee: 1.5% per annum. Performance fee: 20% above a high water mark, with a SOFR + 3% hurdle.

What is the liquidity / redemption terms?

Initial lock‑up: 12 months. Thereafter, quarterly redemptions with 30 days’ notice.

How do I access due diligence materials?

Log into the Secure Investor Portal or request a DDQ from ir@asiprime.com.

Are the strategies audited?

Yes, by a Big‑4 firm annually. Audited financials are available to qualified investors upon request.

Which regulators oversee ASI PRIME?

We are registered with the FCA (UK) and operate in compliance with SEC (US) and ESMA (EU) regulations. Our affiliates hold local licences where required.

How do I get in touch with Investor Relations?

Email ir@asiprime.com or use the contact form.
