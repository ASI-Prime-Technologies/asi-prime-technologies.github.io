Our LegalTech division is headquartered in Yaoundé – the heart of CEMAC’s economic community and a rising hub for digital finance and legal innovation. The location was chosen for three strategic reasons:

  • Cost‑efficient deep talent pool: Yaoundé offers a trained pool of legal engineers and computer scientists at a fraction of European costs, allowing us to scale formal verification without compromising quality.

From Yaoundé, we audit over 500 smart contracts annually and have secured over $40 billion in on‑chain value. Our secondary auditing centres operate in London (common law expertise) and Singapore (APAC regulatory coverage).

Auditing Capabilities

We don’t just review code – we mathematically prove properties, model economic incentives, and map legal structures into enforceable constraints.

Formal Verification

We use interactive theorem proving (Coq, Lean) and SMT solvers (Z3) to prove correctness properties. Our proofs cover reentrancy, access control, arithmetic overflows, and invariants.
