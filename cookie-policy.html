Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026 | Version 2.0

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website recognise your device and remember information about your visit (e.g., your preferred language or login status). This Cookie Policy explains how ASI PRIME Technologies Holdings PLC (“we”, “us”, “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies.

2. Types of cookies we use

Strictly Necessary Cookies
Essential for the website to function (navigation, secure areas, consent storage). Cannot be disabled.
Analytics / Performance Cookies
Collect anonymous information about how visitors use our site (pages visited, time spent). We use this to improve performance. You may opt out.
Functional Cookies
Remember your preferences (language, region, cookie consent). You may disable these, but some features may break.
Targeting / Advertising Cookies
We do NOT use third‑party advertising cookies. However, we may use first‑party targeting to show relevant content to existing investors via email campaigns.

3. Cookies we place

  • cookie_consent: Stores your agreement to our cookie notice (expires after 12 months).
  • session_id: Essential for investor portal authentication (expires when you close your browser).
  • _ga / _gid (Google Analytics): Collect anonymised usage statistics. You can block via browser settings.
  • preferred_language: Remembers your language selection for multilingual content (future feature).

Third‑party cookies: We embed no external social media or ad networks. Maps (Leaflet) do not set cookies unless you interact with them.

4. How to manage or disable cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Delete all existing cookies
  • Block third‑party cookies
  • Block all cookies (may break site functionality)
  • Set preferences for specific sites
