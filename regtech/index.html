The Pre‑Trade Compliance Engine

A real‑time rule engine that translates regulatory text into executable constraints – and enforces them before orders reach the market.

RegMapper AI

NLP engine that ingests legal texts (FCA Handbook, MiFID II, SEC rules) and automatically generates rule‑based constraints with >99% accuracy.

RuleGuard

Sub‑millisecond pre‑trade rule evaluation. Handles 500,000+ checks per second with deterministic latency. Rejects non‑compliant orders instantly.

AuditChain

Immutable audit log with SHA‑256 hashing. Every rule evaluation, override, and decision is stored with cryptographic proof – ready for regulator inspection.
