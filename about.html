Our Firm

Founded in 2018, headquartered in London.

ASI PRIME was established by Charles Mfouapon, a former BlackRock portfolio analyst with dual expertise in quantitative finance and financial regulation. The firm's core insight: algorithmic trading and compliance must be merged at the kernel level, not reconciled after the fact.

Today, we manage systematic strategies across global equities, FX, and futures, with a proprietary pre‑trade compliance engine that embeds FCA/SEC/ESMA rules directly into execution logic. Our client base includes pension funds, sovereign wealth funds, and multi‑strat hedge funds.
