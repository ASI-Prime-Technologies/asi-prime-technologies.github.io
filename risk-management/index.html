Adaptive Risk Engine

Our proprietary risk engine runs continuously across all asset classes. It combines historical VaR, expected shortfall, and stressed Monte Carlo simulations with real‑time market data. The engine is fully integrated with our pre‑trade compliance layer: any order breaching risk limits is rejected before it reaches the exchange.

We support multi‑factor risk decomposition, factor crowding analytics, and liquidity‑adjusted VaR. All calculations are accelerated by custom FPGAs, achieving sub‑100µs latency from order receipt to risk decision.
